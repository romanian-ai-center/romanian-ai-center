About Romanian Center for AI

Leading AI innovation in Romania and beyond

Our Mission

We are dedicated to advancing artificial intelligence research and implementation in Romania, bridging the gap between cutting-edge AI research and practical business applications.

What We Do

We specialize in building sophisticated RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) pipelines that combine the power of large language models with domain-specific knowledge bases. Our expertise spans across all major cloud platforms:

  • Google Cloud Platform (GCP) - Vertex AI, BigQuery, Cloud Functions
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) - SageMaker, Lambda, Bedrock
  • Microsoft Azure - Azure AI, Cognitive Services, Functions

Our Approach

We believe in practical AI solutions that deliver real business value. Our RAG pipelines are designed to:

  • Enhance knowledge retrieval and question-answering systems
  • Improve document understanding and analysis
  • Enable intelligent content generation with factual grounding
  • Scale efficiently across cloud infrastructure

Key Technologies

  • LangChain & LlamaIndex
  • Vector Databases (Pinecone, Weaviate)
  • Transformer Models
  • Embedding Models
  • Cloud ML Services
  • Docker & Kubernetes
